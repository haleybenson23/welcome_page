A LITTLE MORE ABOUT MY JOB

I've been working at The Coffee Bar for little over a year. I work as a barista so I make drinks, and even bake our in-house made pastries. The Coffee Bar is located on the Square in San Marcos, Texas and I love working there. My coworkers are really cool and I like the fast-paced movement of the job. Sometimes it can get crazy, especially on the weekends, when all of the students come in to cram homework and all of the families come in for a day out on the town.
